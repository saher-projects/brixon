Our Story

A Labor of
Love & Coffee

Brixon was born in a small Brooklyn kitchen with nothing but a bag of Ethiopian beans and a dream. We believed great coffee deserved great space — warm, unhurried, alive with the smell of fresh bread.

Today, we're your neighborhood's favorite corner. Every cup we pull is a conversation. Every pastry we bake is a moment worth savoring.
